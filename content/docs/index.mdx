**Lupin** is an autonomous greenhouse robot built on the MIRTE Master V2 (a holonomic
4-mecanum base with a 4-DOF arm). It explores and patrols the rows with Nav2, reads
climate data off AprilTags, finds and grades flowers with YOLO, and streams everything
into a **live digital twin** you can drive from a browser, or by voice.

It is a [ROS 2](https://docs.ros.org/en/humble/) Humble workspace organised as nine
packages, and the *same code runs in Gazebo and on the real robot*, only the
deployment branch and a single drive topic change.

<Callout type="info">
  This site documents the **`hardware` branch**, where most of the implementation runs.
  Commands are split into **Simulation** and **Hardware** tabs wherever they differ.
</Callout>

## How it works

Lupin turns an empty map into a queryable model of a greenhouse in one pipeline:

```text
Explore ──▶ Navigate ──▶ Perceive ──▶ Twin ──▶ HMI / Voice
(frontier   (Nav2 MPPI)  (AprilTag    (/twin/   (:8090
 BFS)                     + YOLO)      state)    console)
```

A mission orchestrator frontier-explores the live SLAM map until it has discovered *N*
AprilTag stations, drives an approach pose at each, reads its climate tag and assesses
its flowers, and publishes every reading as a typed observation. A digital-twin node
aggregates those observations into a latched world snapshot and an on-demand sensor
heatmap, which the web console renders live.

## The stack, page by page

- **[Navigation](/docs/navigation)**, Nav2 tuned for a mecanum base: MPPI controller, Omni motion model, narrow-aisle costmaps, AMCL/SLAM modes.
- **[Mission orchestrator](/docs/mission)**, the hierarchical state machine: exploration → monitoring, per-tag approach poses, pause/E-stop policy.
- **[Perception](/docs/perception)**, AprilTag detection (OpenCV ArUco), YOLO flower detection, and the aggregator that fuses them.
- **[Digital twin](/docs/digital-twin)**, `/twin/state` aggregation and the inverse-distance-weighted `get_field` heatmap service.
- **[Greenhouse bridge](/docs/greenhouse-bridge)**, the ROS wrapper around the course environmental simulator.
- **[Web HMI & voice](/docs/web-hmi)**, the browser mission console and its 16-tool voice agent.
- **[Teleop & manual control](/docs/teleop)**, gamepad/keyboard driving, arm presets, the command-arbitration bus.
